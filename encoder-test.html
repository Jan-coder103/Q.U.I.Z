How to use:

1. Click "Generate Test Image" to create a patterned 512x512 image (or upload your own).

2. Click "Encode" to apply Stage 1 (color inversion) + Stage 2 (block shuffle).

3. Click "Decode" to reverse: Stage 1 (grid unshuffle) + Stage 2 (color re-inversion).

4. The "Decoded" canvas should match the "Original" exactly.

5. Use "Download Scrambled" to save the scrambled image for your images/ folder.

The same Mulberry32 + Fisher-Yates logic is used here as in the PWA's decoder.
